Over three months in 2020, Earth has been overrun by sightless predators of unknown origin who have hypersensitive hearing and indestructible, armored skin. From their attacks on anything that makes noise, human and animal populations are devastatingly few.

To learn the rules of survival in this muted dystopia is essential; nevertheless, an otherwise joyous event is threatening an already frail stability.

All it takes is one noise and everything can go wrong.
